Roulette

the most advanced and most empressive function of the bot is its ability to play roulette. I also programmed a points system that is able to accuratly track all palyers scores and create leaderboards. If the player runs out of points, it also has the !work function that will select a number between 1000 and 10000 at random to give the user.

Usage: !roulette (bet amount) (number/color)

For example: !roulette 1000 red
